A curriculum vitae, commonly referred to as a CV, is typically used for teaching or research opportunities and includes publications, professional presentations, classes taught, and other relevant academic information. A CV has a minimum of two pages, but typically runs much longer. Some companies use “CV” as a synonym for “resume,” often outside the U.S., so make sure to determine employer expectations, research your target country, or speak to a recruiter.
